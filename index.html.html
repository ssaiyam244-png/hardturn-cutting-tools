About Hardturn Cutting Tools

Hardturn Cutting Tools is a trading and distribution company focused on CNC and VMC tooling solutions for the manufacturing industry. We provide a wide range of carbide inserts, end mills, drills, U-drills, and tooling accessories that meet modern machining requirements.

Product Range

Carbide Inserts

Turning, milling and threading inserts for steel, stainless steel and cast iron.

End Mills

High-performance solid carbide end mills for roughing and finishing operations.

Drills

Indexable and solid carbide drills for accurate and stable hole making.

U-Drills

Cost-effective drilling solutions with replaceable inserts.

Tool Holders

BT, CAT and HSK tool holding systems for CNC machines.

Accessories

Screws, shims and spare parts to support continuous machining.
